Cross-Platform Workflows

Leverage multiple AI tools for comprehensive educational solutions

Platform Selection Matrix

Complex Educational Decisions → Claude

  • • Curriculum scope and sequence development
  • • IEP goal development and accommodation planning
  • • Multi-year strategic planning
  • • Budget analysis with multiple variables
  • • Staffing and scheduling optimization

Need Current Information → Gemini or Perplexity

  • • Latest education research and trends
  • • Current legislative changes affecting schools
  • • Recent technology tool comparisons
  • • Grant opportunities and deadlines
  • • Current best practices from other districts

Quick Daily Tasks → ChatGPT

  • • Parent communication emails
  • • Basic lesson plan outlines
  • • Meeting agendas and minutes
  • • Simple data formatting
  • • Newsletter content and announcements

Need Verified Sources → Perplexity

  • • Policy research and legal compliance
  • • Academic research for program decisions
  • • Evaluation of vendor claims
  • • Professional development program vetting
  • • Special education legal requirements

Multi-Platform Research Project Example

PROJECT: New Math Curriculum Implementation for Grades 3-5

Phase 1

Background Research (Perplexity)

"Research current best practices for elementary mathematics curriculum in K-12 education. Focus on: - Peer-reviewed studies from 2022-2025 - Implementation examples from similar districts - Outcome data and effectiveness measures - Cost considerations and resource requirements"

Phase 2

Strategic Analysis (Claude)

"Based on this research [paste Perplexity results], analyze how we could implement a new math curriculum in our district. Consider: - Our specific demographics and needs - Budget constraints of $50,000 - Current staff capacity and expertise - Potential barriers and solutions - Timeline for implementation"

Phase 3

Resource Identification (Gemini)

"Find specific resources, training programs, and materials to support implementing [CLAUDE'S RECOMMENDATIONS]. Include: - Professional development opportunities - Curriculum materials and tools - Technology requirements and costs - Vendor options and comparisons"

Phase 4

Communication Materials (ChatGPT)

"Create stakeholder communication materials for this project: - Executive summary for school board (1 page) - Staff announcement and FAQ (2 pages) - Parent information letter (1 page) - Implementation timeline graphic"

Quality Assurance Checkpoints

Individual Review

Every AI Output:

  • □ Factual accuracy verified
  • □ Alignment with district policies
  • □ Appropriate tone and language
  • □ No confidential information
  • □ Clear attribution if sharing

Peer Review

Important Outputs:

  • □ Colleague accuracy check
  • □ Department head approval
  • □ Legal/HR review if needed
  • □ Special education review

Supervisory Review

Public-Facing Content:

  • □ Principal approval
  • □ Superintendent approval
  • □ Communications review
  • □ Legal counsel if needed
