Quick Start by District Size

Small District

Under 1,000 students

  • • Start with free tiers for most staff
  • • 2-3 paid subscriptions for administrators
  • • Estimated cost: $40-60/month

Medium District

1,000-5,000 students

  • • Mix of free and paid accounts
  • • Department heads get paid access
  • • Estimated cost: $200-400/month

Large District

Over 5,000 students

  • • Consider enterprise solutions
  • • Department-based licensing
  • • Estimated cost: $500-1,500/month
