Classroom Teacher AI Guide

Your Recommended Platform Stack

Primary Tool: ChatGPT

For daily communications and quick tasks

Budget: $20/month (ChatGPT Plus)

  • • Parent emails
  • • Assignment creation
  • • Quick brainstorming

Secondary Tool: Claude

For complex lesson planning

Budget: Free tier or $20/month

  • • Curriculum development
  • • Differentiation strategies
  • • Complex problem-solving

Total Monthly Budget: $20-40 depending on usage

Your Daily AI Workflow

1

Morning (7:00-8:00 AM)

Use ChatGPT for quick parent emails and daily planning adjustments

2

Planning Time

Use Claude for detailed lesson development and differentiation strategies

3

After School

ChatGPT for grading feedback and tomorrow's prep

4

Weekly Research

Perplexity (free) for fact-checking and finding teaching resources

Your Best First Prompt

"I teach [grade level] [subject] and need help with [specific task]. My students include [brief demographics]. Please provide practical, immediately usable suggestions that consider limited classroom resources."
