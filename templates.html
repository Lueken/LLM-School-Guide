Elementary Teacher Templates

Differentiation Strategy

I'm teaching [TOPIC] to [GRADE LEVEL] students. I have [NUMBER] students with these needs:
- [NUMBER] English learners at [PROFICIENCY LEVELS]
- [NUMBER] students with IEPs for [SPECIFIC NEEDS]
- [NUMBER] students reading above/below grade level

Create 3 different approaches to teach this concept:
1. Visual/hands-on approach for kinesthetic learners
2. Simplified version for struggling students
3. Extension activities for advanced learners

Include specific materials and step-by-step instructions.

Positive Parent Communication

Write a positive email to parents about [STUDENT NAME]'s progress in [SUBJECT/BEHAVIOR AREA].

Include:
- Specific example of improvement or achievement
- What we're working on next
- How parents can support at home
- Invitation for questions or conference

Keep tone warm, encouraging, and professional. Length: 150-200 words.

Assessment Creation

Create a [FORMATIVE/SUMMATIVE] assessment for [GRADE LEVEL] [SUBJECT] on [TOPIC].

Include:
- 5 questions at varying difficulty levels
- Clear rubric with 4-point scale
- Accommodation suggestions for special needs
- Extension questions for early finishers

Align to [STATE STANDARDS] and focus on [LEARNING OBJECTIVES].

Administrator Templates

Policy Development

Help me develop a policy for [POLICY AREA] at our [ELEMENTARY/MIDDLE/HIGH] school.

Current situation: [DESCRIBE CURRENT STATE]
Problem to solve: [SPECIFIC ISSUES]
Stakeholders affected: [LIST GROUPS]
Legal considerations: [RELEVANT LAWS/REGULATIONS]
Budget impact: [FINANCIAL CONSTRAINTS]

Create a draft policy that includes:
1. Clear purpose and scope
2. Specific procedures and expectations
3. Implementation timeline
4. Monitoring and evaluation plan
5. Communication strategy for stakeholders

Data Analysis Support

Help me analyze our [ASSESSMENT/PROGRAM] data and create actionable insights.

Data summary: [PASTE DATA OR DESCRIBE RESULTS]
Student demographics: [POPULATION DETAILS]
Comparison points: [PREVIOUS YEARS, OTHER SCHOOLS, BENCHMARKS]

Provide:
1. Key patterns and trends identified
2. Potential root causes for concerning areas
3. Specific intervention recommendations
4. Success celebration opportunities
5. Next steps for improvement planning

Focus on equity and ensuring all students succeed.

Special Education Templates

IEP Goal Development

Create measurable IEP goals for a [GRADE] student with [DISABILITY CATEGORY].

Current performance level: [SPECIFIC DATA]
Area of need: [ACADEMIC/BEHAVIORAL/FUNCTIONAL DOMAIN]
Assessment data: [RELEVANT SCORES/OBSERVATIONS]

Goal should:
- Be measurable with specific criteria
- Include appropriate timeline (usually 1 year)
- Address skill development in natural settings
- Consider least restrictive environment
- Include data collection method

Format as complete IEP goal ready for team review.

Accommodation Planning

A [GRADE LEVEL] student with [DISABILITY/LEARNING DIFFERENCE] is struggling with [SPECIFIC CHALLENGE].

Student strengths: [LIST ABILITIES]
Current supports: [EXISTING ACCOMMODATIONS]
Specific difficulties: [DETAILED CHALLENGES]
Learning environment: [CLASSROOM SETUP, SIZE, ETC.]

Recommend:
1. Specific accommodations for this challenge
2. Implementation strategies for teachers
3. Progress monitoring suggestions
4. Home-school collaboration ideas
5. Technology tools that might help

Focus on maintaining high expectations while providing appropriate support.

Curriculum Specialist Templates

Scope and Sequence Development

Help me develop a scope and sequence for [GRADE LEVEL] [SUBJECT] aligned to [STANDARDS].

Consider:
- Prerequisite skills from [PREVIOUS GRADE]
- Vertical alignment with [NEXT GRADE]
- Integration opportunities with [OTHER SUBJECTS]
- Our district's emphasis on [TEACHING APPROACH]

Include:
- Pacing guide for [NUMBER]-day school year
- Built-in review and assessment windows
- Differentiation considerations
- Resource requirements

Format as a comprehensive planning document.

Professional Development Design

Design a professional development session on [TOPIC] for [AUDIENCE].

Duration: [TIME AVAILABLE]
Participants: [NUMBER AND ROLES]
Current skill level: [BEGINNER/INTERMEDIATE/ADVANCED]
Implementation timeline: [WHEN SKILLS WILL BE USED]

Create:
1. Learning objectives aligned to adult learning principles
2. Engaging activities with hands-on practice
3. Resources and handouts
4. Follow-up support plan
5. Success metrics

Include differentiation for varying experience levels.

Quick Start Templates by Role

Teacher First Prompt

"I teach [grade level] [subject] and need help with [specific task]. My students include [brief demographics]. Please provide practical, immediately usable suggestions that consider limited classroom resources."

Administrator First Prompt

"I need to analyze [situation/decision] for our [school level]. Consider our context: [student population size and demographics]. What approach would you recommend?"

Curriculum Specialist First Prompt

"Help me align [curriculum element] with [state standards] for [grade range]. I need both the theoretical framework and practical implementation strategies."

Special Ed Staff First Prompt

"I'm working with a [grade] student with [disability category] who needs support in [specific area]. Current performance data shows [brief summary]."

Platform-Specific Starting Points

Claude

"I need to analyze [complex educational challenge]. Here's comprehensive context: [detailed background]. Walk me through your reasoning process and consider multiple perspectives."

Gemini

"Find current information about [educational topic] from 2024-2025. Focus on [specific aspect] relevant to [grade/subject]. Include implementation examples from similar districts."

ChatGPT

"[Clear, specific request in first sentence]. Context: [brief essential info]. Format: [exactly how you want output]. Keep under [word count]."

Perplexity

"Research [specific educational topic]. Use only peer-reviewed sources and government reports from 2022-2025. Include methodology and conflicting viewpoints."
