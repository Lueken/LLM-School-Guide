Best First Prompts by Role

Classroom Teacher

"I teach [grade level] [subject] and need help with [specific task]. My students include [brief demographics]. Please provide practical, immediately usable suggestions that consider limited classroom resources."

Administrator

"I need to analyze [situation/decision] for our [school level]. Consider our context: [student population size and demographics]. What approach would you recommend and what implementation challenges should I anticipate?"

Curriculum Specialist

"Help me align [curriculum element] with [state standards] for [grade range]. I need both the theoretical framework and practical implementation strategies for teachers with varying experience levels."

Special Education Staff

"I'm working with a [grade] student with [disability category] who needs support in [specific area]. Current performance data shows [brief summary]. Create evidence-based recommendations that can be implemented in inclusive settings."

Emergency Protocols

Platform Downtime Alternatives

  • • Primary down: Switch to secondary platform from role recommendations
  • • All AI down: Use pre-created templates and human backup
  • • Account locked: Contact IT coordinator immediately
  • • Emergency access: Use shared emergency account if available

Data Privacy Incident Response

  1. 1. Stop using the platform immediately
  2. 2. Document what information was shared
  3. 3. Contact IT coordinator within 2 hours
  4. 4. Complete incident report form
  5. 5. Attend additional privacy training

Cost-Benefit Analysis by District Size

Small District

Under 1,000 students

  • • Start with free tiers
  • • 2-3 paid subscriptions
  • • Focus on administrators
  • • Cost: $40-60/month

Medium District

1,000-5,000 students

  • • Mix free and paid
  • • Department heads get paid
  • • Pilot programs first
  • • Cost: $200-400/month

Large District

Over 5,000 students

  • • Enterprise solutions
  • • Department licensing
  • • Comprehensive training
  • • Cost: $500-1,500/month
